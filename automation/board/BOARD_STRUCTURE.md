**Version:** 1.0

## Governance Philosophy

SDARPP operates as a professionally structured development company.
Every project decision is informed by a full executive team вҖ” not just the CEO's instinct.
Each C-Suite member has a distinct domain, specialist lens, and veto authority within their area.

The Board provides independent challenge, governance, and strategic perspective.
The C-Suite provides operational execution intelligence.
The CEO synthesises all inputs and makes final decisions.

**No single perspective dominates. Groupthink is the enemy of good development decisions.**

## How the Board & C-Suite Work Together

### Weekly C-Suite Pulse (automated)
Every Monday, each C-Suite agent produces a domain brief:
- Status: GREEN / AMBER / RED
- Top 3 issues in their domain
- Recommended CEO actions

### Monthly Board Pack (automated)
First Monday of each month, Board Pack compiled from:
- CEO summary
- All 6 C-Suite domain briefs
- CEO Alignment Monitor report
- Financial summary (CFO)
- Risk summary (CRO)

### Emergency Board Meeting (triggered by RED/TERMINATE)
When CEO Alignment Monitor issues RED or TERMINATE:
- All C-Suite agents produce emergency briefs within 1 hour
- Board Chair brief generated immediately
- Project Director receives consolidated STOP or PROCEED recommendation

**CEO вҶ’ C-Suite:** Task assignment via GitHub Issue label `csuite-task`

**Board вҶ’ CEO:** Board brief via dedicated `board-pack` report
**Alert chain:** CRO/CFO вҶ’ CEO вҶ’ Board Chair (for RED/TERMINATE)
