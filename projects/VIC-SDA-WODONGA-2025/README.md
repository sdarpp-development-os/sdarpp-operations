## Development Model

**Mixed-Use Healthcare Hub** (SDARPP design philosophy)

- Clinical Services: CSSD (Central Sterile Supply), pathology, sterilization
- Medical/Allied Health Hub: Specialist practices, diagnostics
- Clinical Transition Services: Step-down care, patient recuperation
- NDIS SDA Accommodation: 4 × HPS (High Physical Support)
- Private/Supported Accommodation: 16 × VMO (Visiting Medical Officer suites)

**Target:** DSCR 1.25–1.30x across diversified revenue streams
