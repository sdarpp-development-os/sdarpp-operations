## Three Pillars

### Environmental (E)
- **Climate:** Net Zero operations, carbon reduction targets
- **Water:** Conservation, recycling, quality
- **Waste:** Circular economy, landfill diversion
- **Biodiversity:** Biophilic design, habitat enhancement
- **Materials:** Sustainable sourcing, low-carbon

### Social (S)
- **Equity:** Accessibility, inclusive design, employment equity
- **Health:** Participant wellbeing, staff mental health, safety
- **Community:** Aboriginal partnership, local economic benefit
- **Diversity:** Staff representation, procurement diversity
- **Human rights:** Fair labor, dignity, freedom of association

### Governance (G)
- **Board diversity:** Gender, cultural, professional diversity
- **Ethics:** Anti-corruption, whistleblower protection
- **Stakeholder engagement:** Regular consultation, feedback loops
- **Risk management:** Climate risk, regulatory risk, operational risk
- **Transparency:** Regular reporting, public disclosure

## GRI Standards Application

| Topic | Metric | Frequency | Target |
|-------|--------|-----------|--------|
| **Energy** | kWh consumed, % renewable | Monthly | 100% renewable |
| **Emissions** | tCO₂e (Scope 1,2,3) | Annual | Carbon negative |
| **Water** | kL consumed, % recycled | Monthly | 50% reduction |
| **Waste** | tonnes landfill, % diverted | Annual | 80% diversion |
| **Workforce** | FTE employed, % Aboriginal | Annual | 15-20% Aboriginal |
| **Wage equity** | Gender pay gap | Annual | <5% gap |
| **Health & safety** | LTIFR (incidents per 1M hours) | Quarterly | <5 incidents |
| **Community spend** | AUD invested locally | Annual | 10-15% procurement |

## TCFD Climate Risk Framework

### Physical Risks
- Flood/bushfire vulnerability assessment
- Adaptation strategies (resilience design)
- Business continuity planning

### Transition Risks
- Carbon price impact modeling
- Stranded asset potential (low)
- Regulatory compliance costs (moderate)

### Scenario Planning
- 1.5°C warming scenario (action required today)
- 2°C warming scenario (facility designed for resilience)
- 4°C+ scenario (acknowledgement of risks)

---

## Reporting Cadence

- **Quarterly:** Energy, water, waste dashboards
- **Annual:** Full ESG Report (GRI-based)
- **Biennial:** Stakeholder engagement report
- **Every 3 years:** Climate scenario update
