## Design Elements

### Colour Psychology for Healthcare
- **Green:** Healing, reduced stress (primary colour)
- **Blue:** Calm, reduced anxiety (secondary colour)
- **Warm neutrals:** Comfort, safety (wall base)
- **Avoid bright white:** Institutional, harsh

### Infection Control Materials
- **Flooring:** Polished concrete, luxury vinyl (seamless, no grout)
- **Walls:** Ultra-low-VOC paint, hospital-grade finishes
- **Surfaces:** Non-porous counters (quartz, solid surface)
- **Furniture:** Wipeable fabrics, stainless hardware
- **Textiles:** Washable, antimicrobial where appropriate

### Acoustic Design
- **Absorption panels:** Hidden in ceilings, wall treatments
- **Sound masking:** Water features reduce stress from beeps
- **Quiet zones:** Acoustic separation for rest spaces
- **Target:** <45dB ambient noise in occupied spaces

### Circadian Lighting
- **Morning (6am-10am):** Cool, bright (5000K+) to promote alertness
- **Midday (10am-3pm):** Bright (5000K) for engagement
- **Evening (3pm-6pm):** Warm transition (3500K)
- **Night (6pm-10pm):** Dim, warm (2700K or less)
- **All spaces:** Dimmable, occupant control where possible

### Accessibility + Elegance

| Need | Solution |
|------|----------|
| Contrast/wayfinding | Elegant typography, not bold yellow lines |
| Non-slip flooring | Premium materials with suitable texture |
| Grab rails | Designer finishes (brushed brass, timber) |
| Accessible bathroom | Spa-like fixtures, beautiful materials |
| Seating variety | Designer chairs, not institutional |

### Furniture & Fixture Quality
- **Tested durability:** Healthcare-grade, cleanable materials
- **Comfort priority:** Ergonomic, varied options
- **Aesthetic:** Designer selections, not institutional
- **Flexibility:** Modular, reconfigurable spaces
- **Durability spec:** 10-15 year lifespan for upholstery

---

## Wayfinding Excellence
- **Colour zones:** Each area has distinct colour theme
- **Landmarks:** Architectural features guide navigation
- **Signage:** Integrated into design, not applied after
- **Intuitive flow:** No complex navigation required
- **Multiple languages:** Pictographic + written

## Spaces & Functions

| Space | Standards |
|-------|-----------|
| **Consultation room** | Natural light preferred, privacy, comfortable furniture |
| **Waiting area** | Lounge-like seating, varied, entertainment options |
| **Bathroom** | Accessible, beautiful, high-quality fixtures, ventilation |
| **Common areas** | Varied heights (sitting, standing, reclining options) |
| **Bedrooms** | Hotel-quality, personalization encouraged, views |
| **Dining** | Restaurant ambiance, quality furniture, varied seating |
