# Hotel-Style Hospitality Standards for Healthcare Facilities

**Authority:** Marriott Design Standards, 5-Star Hospitality Benchmarks, Healthcare Accessibility Integration  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** 2026-04-05

---

## Purpose

Healthcare facilities don't need to feel like hospitals. SDARPP facilities blend 5-star hospitality comfort with clinical necessity — participants experience dignity, luxury, and care without institutional coldness.

---

## 5-Star Hospitality Benchmarks

### Guest Experience Philosophy
- **Anticipatory service** — staff notice needs before asking
- **Personalization** — remember preferences, customize experience
- **Attention to detail** — quality fabrics, finishes, amenities
- **Proactive problem-solving** — handle issues invisibly
- **Memorable moments** — create positive emotional memories

### Physical Environment Standards

| Element | 5-Star Standard | Healthcare Adaptation |
|---------|-----------------|----------------------|
| **Entrance** | Grand, welcoming, memorable arrival | Accessible, calm, not institutional |
| **Foyer** | Natural light, quality finishes, art | Therapeutic artworks, biophilic elements |
| **Floors** | High-quality materials, warm colours | Slip-resistant, easy-clean, warm palette |
| **Finishes** | Natural materials (wood, stone, leather) | Durable natural materials, warm tones |
| **Furniture** | Luxury, ergonomic, variety of styles | Comfortable, accessible, style variety |
| **Lighting** | Layered (ambient, task, accent), dimmable | Circadian-aligned, dimmable, no glare |
| **Colour** | Warm, sophisticated, seasonal | Calming yet uplifting, therapeutic palette |
| **Sound** | Acoustic comfort, no harsh sounds | Sound-masking, quiet zones, nature sounds |
| **Smell** | Pleasant subtle scents, no artificial | Natural scents (gardens), odour control |
| **Temperature** | Individually adjustable, comfortable range | Accessible controls, comfort priority |

### Service Delivery Standards
- **Guest recognition** — remember names, preferences, routines
- **24/7 availability** — support always available, no hours limitations
- **Problem resolution** — fix issues without requiring guest action
- **Discretion** — provide assistance invisibly, respectfully
- **Flexibility** — adapt to guest needs, preferences, routines

## Integrated Accessibility + Luxury

### Design Principle
**Universal Design doesn't mean institutional. Luxury for everyone.**

### Implementation

| Accessibility Need | Luxury Integration |
|-------------------|-------------------|
| **Wheelchair access** | Curved accessible ramps blend into landscape design |
| **Grab rails** | Designer finishes (brass, wood), integrated into decor |
| **Level flooring** | Premium materials (polished concrete, natural stone) |
| **Wide doorways** | Oversized beautiful doors, quality hardware |
| **Accessible bathrooms** | Spa-like finishes, rainfall showers, luxury fixtures |
| **Service call systems** | Discreet buttons, beautiful design, fast response |
| **Hearing/visual support** | Integrated technology, invisible infrastructure |
| **Mobility support** | Elegant seating variety, not institutional chairs |

## Facility-Specific Applications

### NDIS SDA Accommodation
- **Bedroom quality** — hotel-standard bedding, furnishings, privacy
- **Bathroom experience** — spa-like (quality fixtures, lighting, ventilation)
- **Common areas** — lounge-style living, not institutional seating
- **Dining** — restaurant-quality food, ambiance, service
- **Personal services** — housekeeping, laundry, concierge support

### Aged Care Facilities
- **Lobby** — welcoming, bright, recognizable (not clinical)
- **Dining** — fine dining concept, quality cuisine, choice
- **Recreation spaces** — lounge-style, elegant, multiple options
- **Rooms** — luxury hotel standard, personalization encouraged
- **Services** — attentive staff, proactive care, respect for dignity

### Hospital Outpatient & Clinics
- **Waiting areas** — lounge-like comfort, not seats in rows
- **Consultation rooms** — privacy, comfort, quality finishes
- **Refreshment** — quality tea, coffee, snacks (not vending machines)
- **Wayfinding** — intuitive, beautiful (not institutional signage)
- **Staff interaction** — attentive, unhurried, compassionate

## Wayfinding Excellence

### Principle: Beautiful Navigation, Not Institutional

**Avoid:** Blue/yellow lines, room numbers, clinical signage  
**Implement:** Narrative-based navigation, artistic signage, intuitive flow

### Methods
- **Colour zones** — each area has colour theme (not numbers)
- **Botanical themes** — "Eucalyptus Wing," "Botanical Garden," "Sanctuary Court"
- **Storytelling** — narrative elements guide navigation (tell story while navigating)
- **Landmark design** — distinctive architectural features as wayfinding cues
- **Intuitive flow** — design eliminates need for complex navigation

## Service Delivery Protocols

### Guest Engagement
- **Daily check-ins** — proactive (not reactive) guest engagement
- **Preference documentation** — record preferences, routines, requests
- **Anticipatory service** — fulfill needs before guest asks
- **Problem prevention** — identify issues before they escalate
- **Feedback loops** — gather, act on guest feedback, report changes

### Staff Training
- **Hospitality first** — every staff member trained in guest service
- **Empathy & dignity** — respect for autonomy, choices, preferences
- **Problem-solving** — staff empowered to resolve issues
- **Communication** — clear, respectful, person-first language
- **Excellence** — continuous improvement, quality focus

## Amenities & Services

### Standard Offerings
- **Quality bedding** — Egyptian cotton, premium brands
- **Housekeeping** — daily room service, laundry, turndown
- **Concierge** — support with transportation, activities, requests
- **Dining** — multiple meal options, quality ingredients, dietary accommodation
- **Recreation** — diverse activities, entertainment, engagement
- **Wellness** — gym, massage, relaxation spaces

### Luxury Additions (Project-Dependent)
- **Spa services** — massage, beauty, wellness treatments
- **Fine dining** — restaurant-quality meals, sommelier service
- **Personalized activities** — curated entertainment, learning
- **VIP services** — priority access, customized experiences

## Metrics & Accountability

### Guest Satisfaction
- NPS (Net Promoter Score) target: >70
- Satisfaction surveys: monthly
- Staff training compliance: 100%
- Service issue resolution: 24 hours or less
- Guest retention: >85%
