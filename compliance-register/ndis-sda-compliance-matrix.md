## Compliance Status Legend

| Symbol | Status | Meaning |
|--------|--------|---------|
| ✅ | Compliant | Requirement met, evidence documented |
| ⚠️ | Needs Review | Potential issue requiring architect review |
| ❌ | Non-Compliant | Requirement not met, remediation needed |
| ⭕ | Pending | Awaiting project-specific assessment |

---

## How Projects Use This Matrix

1. **Copy** this checklist into each project's `sda/sda-compliance-checklist.md`
2. **Populate** with project-specific evidence and status
3. **Update** status as design progresses (Concept → Schematic → DD → CD → Construction)
4. **Review** monthly during design phase, weekly during construction
5. **Audit** at practical completion before SDA enrolment
